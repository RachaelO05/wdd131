Thrift Stores

Thrift stores, or second hand stores, that buy and sell pre-loved items at a cheaper price. Thrift stores house a mix of everything. Accessories, formal wear, shoes, vintage clothing and even designer items. Thrift stores in South Africa include: Second chance clothing, The sanctuary thrift and thrift vintage clothes

Markets

Markets are places with many vendors selling different things, food, collectables, crafted goods and clothes. You can find second hand clothes there as well as crafted clothes, such as painted clothing or special crafted clothing. There are markets all over South Africa. You should google some of the closer ones to you and find which have the best clothing to thrift.

Charity Shops

Charity Shops are second hand shops that receive their inventory from donations and the profits they get from selling usually go towards non-profits or charity organization. A bonus on buying from charity shops is you're supporting their organization. Charity shops you can look for in South Africa include the SPCA charity stores, or disabled homes charity shops, or any other charity organization you can find.

Online Thrift Stores

Online thrift stores are similar to normal thrift stores, however users upload their second hand clothing, put a price to it and sell it online. Many people use online thrifting stores as it's more convenient while still being reasonably priced. Online thrift stores that are South African based include: Yaga, ThriftyWays and Love it Again

Overstock stores

Overstock stores are stores that buy overstocked clothing from retail stores and sell them at a discounted price. Although this isn't a second hand store, you can find good qaulity clothes at a discounted price. Overstock stores are relatively new in South Africa, there are some, such as Faro.
